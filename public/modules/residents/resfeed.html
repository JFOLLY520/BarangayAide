BarangayAide
Welcome to BarangayAide! The main feed that will show up is the barangay you have chosen in the registration section. You are able to view other feeds by filtering. You can filter by barangay, issue, or status. The top section of the feed provides you an area to post your complaint. Complaints are manually approved by the barangay. To change your personal details such as email, name, password, and barangay, you can find that in the personal details section. When searching make sure you click the search button after entering the fields you have searched for. Fields that can be searched for are names and description.
